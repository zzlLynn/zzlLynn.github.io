My Fireworks is a joint project of Shanghai Jiao Tong University and Shanghai Science & Technology Museum. I joined the team from the very beginning and was responsible for designing the entire user flow, user interface, and 3D stereoscopic models in augmented reality.

SSTM urgently needs innovative outreach to help it attract visitors even though its exhibit renewal rate is low. The educational role it plays deserves to be better utilized and integrated with textbooks.

Attract visitors to come and have the desire to visit SSTM repeatedly

Combine with students' textbook knowledge, enabling the audience to further understand the science content of the exhibits, thus bringing out the broader value of SSTM

After collecting materials, users create fireworks and can record sounds.

Users scan QR codes on actual souvenirs to view the hybrid ones and share them.
