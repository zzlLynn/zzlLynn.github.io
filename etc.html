XYZ Cosmetic

Package Design

This project is a design for cosmetic self-branding XYZ, and we followed the ESG principle to keep the design and texture while minimizing packaging consumption. In the mockup stage, we chose paperboard and 3D-printed resin as the materials for the outer and inner packaging respectively.
