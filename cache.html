UIST Poster

Tie Memories to E-souvenirs: Hybrid Tangible AR Souvenirs in the Museum

This paper is based on augmented reality technology that combines fireworks experiments with AR models. Multiple interaction methods including mobile user interface, hand gestures and voice are adopted to create a multi-sensory product. This paper promotes the understanding of personalization, socialization and tangible AR.

Graphic Design

Coffee socialization on the chessboards

This project explores the impact of coffee on social interaction through the redesign of ground coffee packaging. The innovative design of the packaging for different sizes of gift boxes is created as different games on a board, thus building social relationships.

Book Design

Reflection of body shame in the weight loss recipe book

This book introduces ingredients commonly used for weight loss to motivate people who are overly dieting to focus on figure issues from a health perspective. It also calls for no body anxiety and shame.
