Overview

Problem

Goals

IWEN is an English reading app designed and developed by a student start-up. In the middle of development, I joined the team. I was given the task of redesigning all the user interfaces, including sorting out the entire information architecture and flow.

The app is overloaded with too many redundant features but loses focus on reading. The navigation and organization are complex and lack signifiers, making it difficult for users to discover the various functions afforded by IWEN.

Identify the reading needs thus deriving the information architecture and simplifying the structure of the tasks

By analyzing the industry, define the core competencies, highlight the brand identity according to the user conceptual model

Solution Preview

By reorganizing the information architecture to streamline the user flow, creating a reading community to enhance user motivation, and adding word books and quizzes to strengthen user reading ability, the design goal is to make users more focused on English reading and improve English learning.

Visual Identity

A new visual identity was created to show the brand's affinity and youthfulness. Every element reflects this idea: the curves of the logomark and the rounded edges.

The logo, just like the brand enables mobile reading to deliver the same textured reading experience as traditional paper books, conveying the concept of creating an immersive reading space. The brand offers a vast range of reading resources and information and hopes that users will have an endless reading experience.

Auxiliary Graphic

The rounded and soft shapes in the logo are extracted to show the comfort and fun that reading brings. The combination of lines and circles helps visual focus, emphasizing that Iwen brings the improvement of focused reading.

Innovative integration of news and book reading

When users open Iwen, the home page first indicates the record of days they have been reading with Iwen, which serves as an incentive.
Iwen offers both news and books to read. Users can choose the proper reading content according to their English reading levels and category preferences. And on the reading page, it provides translation, mode, font, audio, and recording as auxiliary functions.

Record your              English reading learning

The duration of English reading and the number of pages are recorded, thus motivating users to read with acquired words that can be learned repeatedly in the wordbook.

Diversification of reading experience

Users can enjoy a sense of reading satisfaction and companionship by giving their favorite books a like and seeing how many users have read the book. Users can also interact with other readers in the comment section to gain book friends with the same reading taste.
The reading progress bar is displayed, not only to let users know the reading progress and position, but also to motivate them to finish reading the whole book.
