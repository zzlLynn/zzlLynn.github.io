A little about me

I'm studying Information Experience Design at Pratt Institute in

New York. I was born and raised in Shanghai, China, and came to the United State after completing my undergraduate studies in Visual Communication Design at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

Hi there! My name is Lynn. I'm an interdisciplinary designer with

a background in UX design, creative technology, and management. I research, design, and sometimes code. I'm inspired by the community and care about equality. I want to use design as a tool to help foster a culture of equality and sustainability in our society.

When I'm not designing, you can find me cooking Chinese food,

to podcasts, and shooting to capture my life.
