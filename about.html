A little about me

Hey, I'm Lynn. I'm studying Information Experience Design at

Pratt Institute in New York. I was born and raised in Shanghai, China, and came to the US after completing my undergraduate studies in Visual Communication Design at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I like to perceive life and explore the integration of real-world

experience with information design. In terms of what I have explicitly achieved, I have utilized AR to enhance the original museum visit and published a UIST Poster on this topic. A Japan Patent was awarded to me for an ergonomic design of elevator buttons.

When I'm not designing, you can find me cooking Chinese food,

listening to podcasts, and shooting to capture my life.
